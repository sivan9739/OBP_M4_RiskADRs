# Non-Functional Requirements

These quality attributes must be considered during architecture decision-making.

## Security

* Secure by default
* Least privilege access
* Encryption in transit and at rest
* Auditability

## Availability

* High availability where required
* No single points of failure
* Disaster recovery considerations

## Reliability

* Fault tolerance
* Service recovery capabilities
* Operational resilience

## Scalability

* Horizontal scaling preferred
* Support future growth without significant redesign

## Performance

* Meet defined service level objectives
* Support expected workload volumes

## Maintainability

* Clear ownership
* Standardised technologies
* Automated deployment and testing

## Observability

* Centralised logging
* Metrics collection
* Distributed tracing
* Alerting

## Cost

* Optimise infrastructure and operational costs
* Avoid unnecessary complexity

## Compliance

* Meet regulatory and organisational requirements
* Support audit and governance processes
